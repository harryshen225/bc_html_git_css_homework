About Me

A little more than a year ago, we started our discussions about mental health. At the time, we had no idea what kind of reaction we would get. We simply felt compelled to start a conversation.

Since then, the outpouring of stories and support has been overwhelming — this topic touches so many people in so many ways. It’s important we continue the dialogue and work together to remove the stigma. Mental and physical health make up our whole being, and we are at our best when we care for both.
